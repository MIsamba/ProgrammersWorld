Abstraction is simply the process of filtering out and focusing on the more important problem first, out of the smaller more manageable ones.

Example (A real life problem of Musa who wants to organize a party)
Story time!!! (continuation from pattern recognition)

Immediately he arrived home, he went straight to his father to show him what he bought.

Musa; here are the items I bought daddy.

His father; can I have the list?

Musa; Yes sir, here you go.

His father; Musa you have done well, what is remaining now?

Musa; Ok daddy, I haven't yet booked a DJ and a party hall.

His father; Then how can the party be possible Musa? Have you invited your friends!!!

Musa; No daddy, not yet.

His father; I guess you are planning to party on the street!!!, Am I right?

Musa; Sorry sir.

His father; Henceforth Musa, in anything you are doing, make sure you do the more relevant things first, Ok?

Musa; yes daddy.

His father; Now go and send invitation to your friends. I will take care of the party hall, the balloons and the DJ for you.

Musa; Thank you daddy, I am really grateful.

Now read carefully

Remember Musa bought the cake, the drinks and booked a chef first right? But then he forgot that without a party hall, the party cannot be possible.

What musa could have done was to focus on the more relevant things first like a DJ, a party hall and even the decorations. But instead he did the opposite.

In summary, when you have a list of things to accomplish or solve and then you eventually narrow down the list to just solving the important things first. That process is called abstraction.
